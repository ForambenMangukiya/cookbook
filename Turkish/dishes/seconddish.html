Imam Bayildi

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Eggplant (aubergine) 4 medium-sized
Onions 2 medium-sized
Garlic cloves 4
Tomatoes 4 medium-sized
Parsley 1/2 cup chopped
Lemon juice 1/4 cup
Olive oil 1/2 cup
Salt To taste
Prep Time cook Time Total Time Servings
10 minutes 20 minutes 20 minutes 4

Imam Bayildi recipe

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the eggplants in half lengthwise, leaving the stem intact. Score the flesh in a crisscross pattern, sprinkle with salt and let sit for 30 minutes to draw out any bitterness. Rinse with cold water and pat dry. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the onions and sauté until they become translucent, then add the garlic and continue cooking for another minute. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they have broken down and become saucy, stirring occasionally. Add the chopped parsley and lemon juice to the tomato mixture, stir to combine and set aside. Place the eggplant halves in a large baking dish, cut-side up. Spoon the tomato mixture over each eggplant half. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the eggplants are tender and fully cooked. Serve hot or at room temperature, garnished with additional chopped parsley, if desired.
