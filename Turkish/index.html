Turkish Cuisine

Turkish cuisine is a fusion of Central Asian, Middle Eastern, and Mediterranean flavors and techniques. It's known for its rich flavors and use of fresh herbs and spices. Signature dishes include kebabs, stuffed grape leaves, baklava, and Turkish delight. Meze, a selection of small dishes, is a popular way to start a meal. Turkish tea and coffee are also important parts of the culinary culture.
