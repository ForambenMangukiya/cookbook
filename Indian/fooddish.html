Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

Basmati rice 1 cup
oil 1 ½ tablespoons
boneless skinless chicken thighs,
cut into 1-inch chunks 		1 ½ pounds
medium sweet onion, diced ½
tomato paste 3 tablespoons
garlic, minced 3 cloves
freshly grated ginger 1 tablespoon
garam masala 1 ½ teaspoons
chili powder 1 ½ teaspoons
ground turmeric 1 ½ teaspoons
tomato sauce 2 tablespoons
chicken stock 1 cup
Cream ½ cup
chopped fresh cilantro leaves 2 tablespoons
salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
Prep Time cook Time Total Time Servings
10 minutes 20 minutes 20 minutes 4

Chicken Tikka Masala recipes

1. In a large saucepan of 2 cups water, cook rice according to package instructions; set aside.
2. Heat oil in a large stockpot or Dutch oven over medium heat.Season chicken with salt and pepper, to taste. Add chicken and onion to the stockpot and cook until golden, about 4-5 minutes.
3. Stir in tomato paste, garlic, ginger,garam masala,chili powder and turmeric until fragrant, about 1 minute.
4. Stir in tomato sauce and chicken stock; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until reduced and slightly thickened, about 10 minutes. 5. Stir in heavy cream until heated through, about 1 minute. 6. Serve immediately with rice, garnished with cilantro, if desired.
